What can you expect during and after your laser lipolysis?

The procedure begins with the application of local anesthesia to ensure your comfort. Dr. Tatiana Leal will make small incisions of approximately 0.5 cm, through which she introduces the laser to precisely break down the fat cells. Over the next 60-90 minutes, the laser works with precision to permanently dissolve the fat cells.

You will see visible results immediately, with a notable reduction in volume from the first day. Results continue to improve over the next 3 months as your body naturally eliminates the treated fat cells. Most of our patients return to their normal activities within 48-72 hours, with minimal recovery time.

As an additional benefit, the thermal effect of the laser stimulates collagen production and improves the extracellular matrix, resulting in a noticeable improvement in skin laxity in the treated areas.

Who should not undergo laser lipolysis?

Although laser lipolysis is a safe procedure, there are some important contraindications:

  • Pregnant or breastfeeding women
  • Minors
  • People with diabetes
  • Patients with pacemakers or electronic medical implants
  • People with lymphatic disorders
  • Patients with skin conditions such as psoriasis or eczema

It is essential that during your evaluation consultation, you inform Dr. Tatiana Leal about any medical conditions or illnesses you currently have. Only then can she determine if this treatment is safe and effective for you.
