PDO threads: Immediate lifting effect without surgery or scars

Have you noticed how time has left its mark on your face? Skin laxity can affect your confidence, but PDO threads offer an innovative solution in just 20 minutes, without the need for surgery.

Polydioxanone (PDO) threads represent a revolution in facial rejuvenation. Through intradermal or subcutaneous application using a specialized microcannula, these threads not only lift and tighten tissues immediately but also generate biostimulation, progressively improving the quality and firmness of your skin.

The most surprising part: the procedure is performed through a single entry point per thread, with no incisions or visible scars, allowing you to quickly return to your social life.

Your experience with PDO threads: Fast, safe, and comfortable

Your comfort is our priority during the PDO thread treatment. The procedure begins with a detailed evaluation of your face, identifying the specific areas that require correction of skin laxity. We apply local anesthesia to ensure you feel no discomfort during the procedure.

Over the next 20 minutes, Dr. Tatiana Leal performs the precise insertion of the PDO threads using a microcannula, an advanced technique that allows each thread to be introduced through a single entry point. This method avoids incisions, minimizes the possibility of bruising, and eliminates the risk of visible scars.

Once finished, you will notice an immediate lifting effect. The most surprising part for our patients is that the swelling usually disappears completely within 3 days, allowing you to quickly return to your social and professional life, showcasing a naturally rejuvenated face.

In which areas can PDO threads be applied?

PDO threads are extremely versatile and can be applied in multiple areas to correct skin laxity and improve facial contours:

  • Mid-face: lifting of cheekbones and cheeks
  • Facial oval: redefinition of the contour and jawline
  • Neck: correction of sagging and horizontal lines
  • Double chin: reduction and definition of the submental area
  • Eyebrows: natural lifting of the brow arch
  • Nasolabial folds: softening of expression lines

During your evaluation consultation, we will assess together which areas would benefit most from this treatment in your specific case.
