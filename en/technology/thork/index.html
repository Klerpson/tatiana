Frequently Asked Questions about Thork

Thork is a Brazilian shock wave technology that uses pulsed mechanical energy to stimulate body tissue. The treatment works by applying acoustic waves that penetrate the tissues, activating collagen production, improving blood circulation, and accelerating the body's natural recovery processes.

Thork treatment is generally well-tolerated. Patients experience a rhythmic tapping sensation during the session, which is adjusted according to individual tolerance. The intensity is personalized to ensure comfort while maintaining treatment effectiveness.

The number of sessions varies depending on the treatment goal and condition being treated. For sports results, weekly sessions for 4-6 weeks are recommended. For aesthetic treatments, the plan may extend to 8-12 sessions. We develop a personalized protocol based on your specific needs.

Thork is versatile and effective for multiple applications. In sports, it treats muscle injuries, tendinitis, and accelerates post-training recovery. In aesthetics, it's effective for reducing cellulitis, improving skin firmness, and enhancing other body treatments.
