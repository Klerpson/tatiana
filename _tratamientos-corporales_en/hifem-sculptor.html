What is the Sculptor treatment?

HIFEM Technology: The revolution in muscle toning

The Sculptor treatment uses HIFEM (High-Intensity Focused Electromagnetic) technology to generate supramaximal muscle contractions, which are impossible to achieve with voluntary action. These contractions cause extreme stress on muscle fibers, forcing them to adapt and grow, resulting in increased muscle volume.

Unlike other treatments, Sculptor is 100% non-invasive and is performed using 4 applicators (two large and two medium) that are placed on the skin. These applicators allow for working on two body areas simultaneously, maximizing treatment efficiency.

During the procedure, patients experience intense muscle contractions while resting comfortably. There is no pain, just the sensation of a high-intensity workout, but without the associated physical effort.
