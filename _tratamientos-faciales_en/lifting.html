Why choose a non-surgical facelift?

Technology that surpasses traditional surgery

While surgery mechanically pulls tissues, the non-surgical facelift works with your skin's natural mechanisms. HIFU and radiofrequency stimulate collagen production from within, achieving a progressive and natural firming effect. Think of it as awakening your skin's own rejuvenating potential, similar to how a great workout tones your body naturally.

During your complimentary consultation, we'll analyze your facial structure and design a customized protocol combining the most appropriate technologies for your needs. We'll show you exactly how each treatment will work to achieve your desired transformation, and can help coordinate your visit if you're traveling from abroad.

Your non-surgical facelift experience

Treatment begins with a detailed skin assessment and application of a special conducting gel. Over the next 45-60 minutes, while you relax in our state-of-the-art facility, the different technologies work in synergy to stimulate collagen and elastin production in your skin's deep layers.

Most patients describe a tolerable warming sensation during the procedure. You might experience slight redness that resolves within hours - perfect for a Thursday treatment to look fresh for weekend events. Best of all: you can return to your daily activities immediately, making it ideal for busy professionals.
